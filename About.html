About

At Numbertarian, we believe numbers are more than just digits—they’re the foundation of everyday decisions, big ideas, and the way we understand the world. Founded by Patrick, our mission is to empower people through intuitive, reliable calculator apps that make math accessible, efficient, and even enjoyable. Whether you're crunching numbers for school, work, or daily life, Numbertarian is here to help you make sense of it all—one calculation at a time.
